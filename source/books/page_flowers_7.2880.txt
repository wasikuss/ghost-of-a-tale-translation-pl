Harvest's end. For the first time in an age Master Lewlin sang, his voice bright and clear. And you knew then the time had come ~ to travel south, with the winter at your tail ~ to leave the valley, the village, and Merra. 
Merra; you longed for her to join you on the road, but you knew it was impossible, for her brothers were too young to work the fields, and her father was too frail. She was duty~bound to them, as you were to your master.
