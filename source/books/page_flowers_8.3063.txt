Your mentor or Merra; to choose between them was unthinkable. "Tomorrow will be the day," you told yourself, as you'd done yesterday, and the day before. You slept in fits and starts, rising before dawn no closer to a decision.
Master Lewlin's room was empty, blankets neatly folded at the foot of the bed. On the table was a lute ~ his lute, made by his master from driftwood gathered on the shore of Lake Vaelia, and a note ~ "this life is your song".
