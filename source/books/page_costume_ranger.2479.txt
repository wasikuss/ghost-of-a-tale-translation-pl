Rangers are mercenaries, skilled in tracking and a common sight in garrisons such as Great Birham and Dwindling Heights. The soldiers of the Rat Guard are under strict orders to let rangers go about their business unhindered; it is an uneasy truce.

<b>A ranger is always seen wearing:</b>

<i>Ranger hood
Ranger mask
Ranger tunic
Ranger belt
Ranger boots</i>
