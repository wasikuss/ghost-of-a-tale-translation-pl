The armour of Lady Duinlan of Dhall, forged of some unknown alloy by the capital's finest artisans. Though it is almost a thousand years old now, the quality of work remains unsurpassed. It was this suit, worn by Duinlan during The Battle of Periclave, that earned her the name "The Gilded Warrior".

<b>The following pieces make up the complete suit:</b>

<i>Gilded helmet
Gilded chainmail face guard
Gilded chest plate & pauldrons
Gilded faulds
Gilded greaves</i>
